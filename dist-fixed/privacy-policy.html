1. Introduction

Welcome to Zuzu Sport ("the App"), operated by Barshaf Investments Ltd. Your privacy and your child's safety are our top priorities. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy. We are committed to COPPA compliance and ensuring a safe experience for children.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our fitness services:

  • Account Information: Parent's email address for account creation and communication
  • Child's Profile: First name, age range, and fitness preferences (no last names or personal identifiers)
  • Usage Data: Workout completion, progress tracking, and app usage statistics
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app compatibility

We DO NOT collect: Full names, addresses, phone numbers, photos, or any personal identifiers of children.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide personalized fitness activities for your child
  • Track progress and maintain workout streaks
  • Send important updates about the app to parents
  • Improve our fitness content and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested

4. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect the safety of our users
  • With your explicit consent for specific services

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only
  • Secure servers with backup and recovery systems

6. COPPA Compliance

As required by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We require parental consent before collecting any information from children under 13
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time
  • We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services
  • Children cannot publicly post or share personal information

7. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review what information we have collected about your child
  • Request deletion of your child's account and all associated data
  • Refuse further collection of your child's information
  • Control your child's app usage and settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@zuzusport.co.il

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account, we will permanently delete all personal information within 30 days.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify parents of any significant changes via email and post updates on this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

10. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your child's data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@zuzusport.co.il
Support: help@zuzusport.co.il
