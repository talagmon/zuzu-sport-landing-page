Child Safety & COPPA Compliance

Zuzu Sport is committed to creating the safest possible environment for children and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Important: Parental Supervision Required

Children under 13 must have parental permission and supervision to use Zuzu Sport. Learn about our parental controls →

What is COPPA?

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) is a U.S. federal law that requires special protections for children under 13 years old.

Data Collection Limits

We only collect the minimum necessary information and never collect personal data from children without verifiable parental consent.

Parental Consent

Parents must provide verifiable consent before their child can use features that collect personal information.

Data Protection

All collected data is encrypted, securely stored, and never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Parental Controls & Oversight

We provide comprehensive tools for parents to monitor and control their child's experience.

Account Verification

Parents must create and verify their account before children can access any features that collect personal information.

Activity Monitoring

Parents can view their child's activity history, progress reports, and time spent using the app.

Privacy Settings

Comprehensive privacy controls allow parents to customize what information is collected and how it's used.

Data Deletion Rights

Parents can request deletion of their child's data at any time through our simple data deletion process.

Request Parental Access

We will respond to your request within 10 business days and may require additional verification.

Child Safety Features

Our app is designed with multiple layers of protection to ensure a safe experience for children.

No External Links

The app contains no external links that could take children away from the safe environment.

No Ads

Zero advertisements ensure children are not exposed to inappropriate content or tracking.

No Chat Features

No communication features prevent any possibility of contact with unknown individuals.

Expert Content

All content is created and reviewed by certified child fitness and safety experts.
